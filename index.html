FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries

Are you an authorized Epson and Canon service center?

Yes, New Generation Computer is an officially authorized service center for both Epson and Canon printers. We use genuine parts and have certified technicians.

Do you provide on-site repair services?

Yes, we offer convenient on-site printer and computer repair services across Akola, Washim, and Buldhana districts.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Complex issues may take longer, but we always keep you informed about the timeline.

Do you repair printers of all brands?

Yes, while we are authorized for Epson and Canon, we also repair HP, Brother, Samsung, and all other major printer brands.

What is your cartridge refilling service?

We provide professional ink and toner cartridge refilling services for all printer brands at affordable prices with quality ink.

Do you offer AMC for printers?

Yes, we offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for printers with regular servicing, priority support, and discounted repairs.
